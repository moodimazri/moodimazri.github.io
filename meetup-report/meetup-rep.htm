SFHTML5 Meetup!

What is HTML5 & what is the objective of this meetup?

This meetup is the largest web development meetup in the world! It is hosted monthly, sometimes at google actual office location in the Financial District of San Francisco, & sometimes it is held in different locations such as hotels. Industry leaders in technology present this meetup. They cover different fields such as, the latest HTML & css, javascript, Ruby on Rails, or it could be any language used as an architecture or framework for companies. Every event aims mainly to network between diverse people coming from different companies, schools or backgrounds. The event provides a demo with most recent technology trend through a nice atmosphere created with dinner, snacks, drinks & music!

What is this meetup is about?

This time the SFHTML5 meetup hosted students from Howset University in Belgium. It is one of the top universities in web development and technology. These students basically came to network with people from industry leaders such as Airbnb, Microsoft, Pinterest, and of course google developers. Interestingly, some of those leaders were at Hawset University, and attended the same meetup five years ago!Hence, these leaders emphasise the importance of these meetups and how it created a dream path for them to pursue! Industry leader such as Airbnb, has probably the biggest portion of the pie! One of airbnb developers "Jens Vanderhaeghe" presented the architecture and the framework of airbnb system and how they started. He mainly described three things: (1) the product drivers that precipitated the changes, (2) the steps they took to move away from the legacy Rails solutions, and (3) some of the key pillars of the new stack. In terms of students, they presented their amazing projects that incorporated pure physical engineering work, and programing. As an example of two drum sticks that resemble real drums playing, Stijn &Sebastiaan. Another, project about fertility, and what are the risk combined with having late children by, Laurens &Mike.

Things that I learn from this meetups!

The meetup gathering and the social interaction are an amazing culture that exist in San Francisco! You can join as much as you want, and walk out with a bunch of information, and business cards. I had the oportiunity to meet with some developers and talk about their work and how they started. I was impressed by these industry leaders people such as Airbnb and Pinterest. Having talked to them, I realized that their hard work and persistent are what make them successful! Also, meeting with other students from Hawset University and having the opportunity to talk about their future prospect, gave me an insight of how all successful entrepreneur starts and how they think about the future.
